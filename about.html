About Me

Hello! I am Esha Navarkar a Product Designer and Researcher currently pursuing my Master’s in Information Experience Design at Pratt Institute, New York.

My design philosophy centers on empathy and meaningful user experiences. I combine creativity with research-driven methods to explore how design can promote learning, accessibility, and social connection.

Design Journey

  • Presented paper"Beyond Playthings"at the International Conference on Research into Design (ICoRD’25).
  • Co-facilitated Design Thinking and Innovation workshops for students in grades 8–10.
  • Designed educational packaging for global clients and developed creative curricula for design aspirants.

Beyond Design

When I am not designing- I paint, sketch and host art workshops under my brand DOTS. I believe that creativity across mediums helps me connect ideas in unexpected ways.
